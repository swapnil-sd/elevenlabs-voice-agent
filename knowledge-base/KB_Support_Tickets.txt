## Performance/Speed Tickets (1,012 tickets - Biggest Category)

### Breakdown  
- Dashboard loading slow: 487 tickets (48% of category)  
- Report generation timeout: 189 tickets (19%)  
- Task list lag with 100+ items: 156 tickets (15%)  
- File upload slow/failing: 98 tickets (10%)  
- General app is slow complaints: 82 tickets (8%)

### Affected User Segments  
- Enterprise users file 58% of performance tickets despite being only 23% of user base  
- The issue disproportionately affects accounts with 50+ projects  
- Performance tickets spike between 9-11 AM ET (likely correlated with morning standup prep)

### Escalation Rate  
- 34% of performance tickets escalate to Tier 2 (engineering investigation)  
- 8% escalate to Tier 3 (require backend team fix)  
- Average escalation resolution: 5.2 business days

### Customer Impact  
- 12 Enterprise accounts (representing $340,000 ARR) have explicitly threatened to churn due to performance issues  
- 3 Enterprise accounts have already downgraded from annual to monthly billing (churn risk signal)  
- Support team estimates 15-20% of performance tickets come from users who will not renew

## Mobile App Issues (798 tickets - Second Biggest Category)

### Breakdown  
- Android crash on project switch: 312 tickets (39%)  
- iOS UI rendering issues: 145 tickets (18%)  
- Sync failures (offline to online): 134 tickets (17%)  
- Push notifications not working: 98 tickets (12%)  
- Login session expiring too frequently: 67 tickets (8%)  
- Other mobile bugs: 42 tickets (5%)

### Platform Split  
- Android: 523 tickets (66%)  
- iOS: 275 tickets (34%)

### The Android Crash Issue (Deep Dive)  
- First reported: December 15, 2025  
- Reproduction rate: approximately 70% of Android users on TaskFlow v4.2.1  
- Root cause identified by engineering: memory leak in project switching logic introduced in v4.2.0 update  
- Fix status: patch v4.2.2 submitted to Play Store on February 20, currently in review  
- Expected resolution: March 5, 2026  
- Workaround communicated to users: force-close and reopen the app before switching projects

---

## Search Problems (534 tickets - Third Biggest Category)

### Breakdown  
- Search not finding tasks (task exists but search returns no results): 223 tickets (42%)  
- Cannot search inside comments: 156 tickets (29%)  
- No cross-project search: 89 tickets (17%)  
- Search results not sortable/filterable: 66 tickets (12%)

### Engineering Assessment  
- Current search uses basic text matching on task titles and descriptions only  
- Comments, attachments, and custom fields are NOT indexed  
- Search index rebuilds nightly so tasks created during the day may not appear in search until the next day  
- Engineering has proposed migrating to Elasticsearch, estimated 6-8 weeks of work, currently in backlog (not yet prioritized)

## Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) for Support

- Overall CSAT: 78% (down from 84% in Q4 2025\)  
- CSAT by Channel: Live Chat 82%, Email 76%, Phone 71%  
- First Response Time (average): 2.4 hours (target: under 2 hours)  
- First Contact Resolution Rate: 62% (target: 70%)

### Why CSAT Is Declining  
- 41% of dissatisfied respondents cite issue not resolved as the reason  
- 29% cite had to repeat my problem multiple times  
- 18% cite response was too slow  
- 12% cite agent did not understand my issue

## Churn Risk Signals from Support Data

### High-Risk Accounts (based on ticket volume \+ sentiment analysis)  
- 28 accounts flagged as high churn risk  
- Combined ARR of flagged accounts: $892,000  
- Common pattern: 3+ tickets in 30 days with negative sentiment, typically about performance or mobile crashes

### Top 5 At-Risk Enterprise Accounts  
1\. Acme Corp ($120K ARR) - 14 tickets in February, all about dashboard performance. Account champion has stopped responding to CSM emails.  
2\. GlobalTech ($95K ARR) - Filed formal complaint about Android crashes. Evaluating competitor (Monday.com).  
3\. Pinnacle Systems ($88K ARR) - Downgraded from annual to monthly billing. Cited loss of confidence in product quality.  
4\. DataDrive Inc ($78K ARR) - Support tickets escalating in tone. Latest ticket: If this is not fixed by March 15, we are leaving.  
5\. Brightpath ($67K ARR) - Reduced seat count from 150 to 80\. PM told CSM they are parallel testing alternatives.
