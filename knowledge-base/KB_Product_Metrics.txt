## Engagement Metrics

### Daily Active Users (DAU)  
- February Average DAU: 28,400  
- January Average DAU: 29,800  
- DAU/MAU Ratio: 42% (healthy range: 40-60%)  
- DAU trend: slight decline (-4.7% month-over-month)

### Weekly Active Users (WAU)  
- February Average WAU: 47,200

### Feature Usage (February 2026\)  
| Feature | Weekly Active Users | % of WAU | Trend |  
|---------|-------------------|---------|-------|  
| Task Creation | 41,200 | 87% | Stable |  
| Kanban Board | 33,900 | 72% | Up 8% (launched Dec 2025\) |  
| Comments/Collaboration | 29,400 | 62% | Stable |

### Key Engagement Observations  
- Dashboard usage is declining because users are frustrated with slow loading times. They are finding workarounds (spreadsheets, Slack updates).  
- Search usage is declining because users have lost confidence in search quality. Several interview participants mentioned using browser search (Ctrl+F) instead.  
- Mobile app usage is declining sharply due to the Android crash bug. Field teams are reverting to desktop-only usage.  
- Kanban board is the bright spot — it is the fastest-growing feature since its December launch.  
- Integration usage is growing steadily, especially the Slack integration.

## Conversion Funnel

### Free to Paid Conversion  
- Free users who activate (complete onboarding): 62%  
- Activated users who hit "aha moment" (create 5+ tasks AND invite 1+ team member): 34%  
- Aha moment users who convert to paid within 30 days: 24.7%  
- Overall free-to-paid conversion: 8.4%  
- Conversion rate has declined from 11.2% in Q4 2025

### Why Conversion Is Declining  
- Analysis of users who signed up but did not convert (surveyed 200 users):  
  - 38% said they hit performance issues during the trial and lost confidence  
  - 24% said the mobile experience was too buggy  
  - 19% said a competitor offered a better free tier  
  - 12% said they did not understand the value of Pro features  
  - 7% said pricing was too high

### Trial-to-Paid Timeline  
- Average days from signup to paid conversion: 14.3 days (was 11.8 in Q4 2025\)  
- Users are taking longer to convert, suggesting hesitation

## Competitive Landscape (from Sales Intelligence)

### Win/Loss Analysis (Jan-Feb 2026\)  
- Competitive deals won: 67  
- Competitive deals lost: 43  
- Win rate: 61% (down from 68% in Q4 2025\)

### Top Competitors in Lost Deals  
1\. Monday.com — 18 lost deals (42% of losses). Cited: better performance, more polished mobile app  
2\. Asana — 12 lost deals (28%). Cited: better enterprise features, portfolio management  
3\. ClickUp — 8 lost deals (19%). Cited: lower pricing, more features in free tier  
4\. Notion — 5 lost deals (12%). Cited: more flexible, all-in-one workspace

### Competitive Threats  
- Monday.com launched a major mobile app update in January 2026 with offline mode and real-time sync. This directly addresses our mobile weakness.  
- Asana released a free tier expansion in February 2026, increasing free plan limits. This threatens our conversion funnel.  
- ClickUp is aggressively pricing enterprise deals at 30-40% below our rates.
