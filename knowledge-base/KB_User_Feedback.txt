## User Interview Insights (12 interviews conducted in February 2026\)

### Interview Participants  
- 4 Free tier users (small teams, 2-5 people)  
- 4 Pro plan users (mid-size teams, 10-30 people)  
- 4 Enterprise users (large organizations, 100+ people)

### Key Finding 1: Dashboard Performance Is the #1 Pain Point  
- 9 of 12 users mentioned dashboard loading time as a frustration  
- Enterprise users report 8-12 second load times for dashboards with 50+ projects  
- Pro users report 4-6 second load times  
- Quote from interview P7 (Enterprise PM): "I've started using spreadsheets for my morning standup because the dashboard takes too long to load. That defeats the purpose of the tool."  
- Quote from interview P3 (Pro user): "It wasn't this slow six months ago. Something changed and it's getting worse."

### Key Finding 2: Search Is Fundamentally Broken for Power Users  
- 7 of 12 users expressed frustration with search  
- The search function does not search inside comments, only task titles and descriptions  
- Users cannot search across multiple projects simultaneously  
- No filtering by date range, assignee, or status in search results  
- Quote from interview P11 (Enterprise TPM): "I have 200 tasks across 8 projects. Finding anything is like searching for a needle in a haystack."  
- Quote from interview P5 (Pro user): "I've resorted to using browser Ctrl+F instead of the built-in search. That tells you everything."

### Key Finding 3: Mobile Experience Is a Dealbreaker for Field Teams  
- 5 of 12 users mentioned mobile issues  
- Android app crashes approximately once per session when switching between projects  
- iOS app is more stable but the UI is not optimized for smaller screens  
- Offline mode does not sync reliably — changes made offline sometimes get lost  
- Quote from interview P9 (Enterprise user): "Our field team has basically stopped using the mobile app. They just wait until they're at a desk."

### Key Finding 4: Notification Fatigue Is Driving Disengagement  
- 6 of 12 users mentioned notification overload  
- Default settings send email for every comment, status change, and assignment  
- Users who turn off notifications miss important updates  
- No option for "digest" mode (daily summary instead of real-time alerts)  
- Quote from interview P2 (Free user): "I turned off all notifications and now I miss deadlines. But when they were on, I was getting 50 emails a day."

### Key Finding 5: Users Love the Collaboration Features  
- 11 of 12 users praised real-time collaboration  
- The commenting system, @mentions, and activity feed are rated highly  
- Teams report that TaskFlow has replaced 60-70% of their project-related Slack messages  
- Quote from interview P8 (Pro user): "The collaboration features are why we stay. Everything else has alternatives, but this is the best team workspace we've used."
